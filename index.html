01. About Me

Hi, I'm Kerem. Since childhood, I’ve been passionate about games and computers. I used to spend hours exploring new worlds and creating my own stories with pure curiosity and imagination. Today, I turn that childhood passion into interactive experiences and games that bring people together.

As a freelance game developer and 3D artist, I create projects in Unity and Unreal Engine, and design characters and environments with Blender. I love experimenting with game mechanics, learning new skills, and making things that entertain and inspire others.

My background is quite versatile: I have degrees in Computer Engineering and Civil Engineering. Currently, I'm in the thesis stage of my master's in Game and Interaction Technologies at Istanbul Technical University (ITU). I enjoy bringing what I’ve learned from different disciplines into game development and teamwork.

Recently, I've been interested in:
  • Unity (C#)
  • Unreal Engine
  • Blender
  • Game/Level Design
  • HTML & CSS

In short, I'm always learning, trying, and sharing. My goal is not just to make games, but to create memories and positive feelings for people.
